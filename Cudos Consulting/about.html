The cudos Philosophy

Why Cudos is different

Carla has an exceptional ability to see through organisational complexity and identify gaps between the brand promise and experiences of customers. She is committed to helping organisations develop action plans that address these gaps, creating a customer-centric culture that delivers customer value.

Developing CX Programmes

Carla has experience of developing CX programmes for commercial and not for profit organisations, in B2B, B2C and B2B2C environments.

12 years experience

Fully qualified
