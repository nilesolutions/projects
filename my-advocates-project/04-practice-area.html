Cerebral Palsy: Uncovering Serious Medical Errors

Our medical and legal staff has helped over 20,000 families by investigating their child’s cerebral palsy. This requires true expertise and understanding of law and medicine. We have even uncovered negligence that other law firms missed. Answer these brief questions if you suspect that a birth injury caused your child’s cerebral palsy. There is no cost for this service. Our medical/legal team will review the information to see if we can help you.

Finding the Truth

After your child is diagnosed with cerebral palsy, don’t delay in contacting us. Some states set deadlines for filing a suit and valuable documents may be lost. Litigation may be the only way to determine the truth and receive compensation. If you suspect a birth injury, call us toll free at 1-877-MyAdvocates (1-877-823-0662) or fill out a brief questionnaire.

Providing Compensation for Families

Major settlements and verdicts from our cerebral palsy cases have provided compensation that will take care of these children for the rest of their lives. Among the cases are:

  • Missed, ignored or misinterpreted warning signs on fetal heart monitors – More information on Electronic Fetal Monitoring
  • Unnecessarily delayed C-sections or failure to perform a C-section
  • Failure to resuscitate a newborn needing help breathing
  • Failure to diagnose or treat an infection
  • Negligent application of vacuum extractor equipment or forceps
  • Misdiagnosis of fetal position in the uterus
  • Severe jaundice (kernicterus)
