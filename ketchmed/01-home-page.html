Our goal is simple

At KetchMedical. our goal is to have a free and open platform for all psychiatric clinicians and employers to efficiently find each other.

We collect, organize, and post all available psychiatric jobs in the U.S.. so you can search and filter according to your preference.

learn more

  • Permanent Employment

    Find permanent employment for all psychiatricjobs/job candidates filtered and sorted per region.

  • Locums, Telepsychiatry, Moonlighting

    Find or post local moonlighting/shift, locum opportunities, as well as Telepsychiatry through our easy online calendar job posting and clinician sign up

  • Mid-level Psychiatric Training

    Whether you are looking for comprehensive psychiatric training or are an employer in an underserved region, we provide intensive psychiatry training to local mid-level clinicians in your region that you can hire or to your existing mid-level staff, in order to ensure that any healthcare organization can staff their positions and be guaranteed to provide evidence based psychiatric care by well trained clinicians.

Solve all of your Job Search Needs in One Place

Psychiatrists, PMHNPs, Nurse Practitioners, Physician Assistants:

  • We collect, filter, and sort all available psychiatric jobs in the U.S. according to the factors that matter most to you
  • Only you control who you share your information with and when, we don’t share your information with anyone
  • Connect only with jobs that fit exactly what you want, region, schedule, pay rate
  • Filter local moonlighting/shift, locum, or telepsychiatry opportunities where and when you want
  • No middleman between you and the employer
  • Primary care and midlevel clinicians: comprehensive psychiatric training available for your next position
learn more

Employers:

  • Search permanent job candidates, post your jobs, and hire all for free
  • Find temporary/locum, moonlighting, or telepsychiatry coverage. Post your position and candidates sign up
  • Credentialing provided
  • Negotiate fees directly with the candidate, no third party involved
  • If your region lacks trained psychiatric clinicians, intensive psychiatric training to local midlevel clinicians (N P/PAs) or primary care physicians is available to staff your open positions
